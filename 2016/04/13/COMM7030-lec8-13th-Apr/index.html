The Cultivation Differential (1)

Objective reality: 1% of all males have jobs related to law enforcement (census)

人口普查
Mediated reality: 12% on TV (from content analysis)
電視調查
Perceived reality: 5% (TV) and 1% (real) (survey questionnaire)
做survey調查，如果1%則沒有受電視影響，5%則有影響

用百分比去減，得出Cultivation Differential，heave viewer are more easily affected.

First-order effect (direct, crime-related)
Census: 0.32 violent crimes per 100 persons
TV: 66%
Survey: number of people involved in violence each week “1 in 10” or “1 in 100?”
Response of 1 in 10: 39% light viewers, 52% heavy viewers
52% - 39% = 13% (Cultivation Differential)

Second-order effect (indirect, more general)
