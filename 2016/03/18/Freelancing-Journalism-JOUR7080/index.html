Is freelance journalism fast replacing foreign correspondence?

Freelance journalism is to make a living in the media, but independent of it, while retaining as much copyright and editorial control over your work as possible.

Freelance reporting is a form of media trade, but without accepting authority of the news industry’s publishers and its corporate arm.

You write a story and someone elsewhere may like it.

How to be a freelancer?

You can engage in the following commissioned work:

  1. Writing feature articles and/or blogs;
  2. Crewing photographer/videographer;
  3. …

Is freelanceing cool?

Former British PM, Churchill, was a freelance international/foreign journalist.
Sixteen former state leaders were either foreign corr or international freelancers.

Freelancers are NOT stringers(特約記者). To be a “Stringer”, suggests a level of professionalism or acceptance of terms and regulatory frameworks set by institutions and media.
