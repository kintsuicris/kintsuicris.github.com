Materialism

Posted on 2016-02-29   |   In Lecture

Prisoner’s Dilemma

Example 01:

2 points or 6 points or 0 point (if over 10% of class choose 6 points).

Usually more than a half will choose 6.

Example 02:

Suspects A and B.
1) Both released if A and B both cooperate;
2) A served 10 years if B betray, vice verse;
3) 5 years both if A and B betray each other;

Mostly 5 years as a result.

Conclusion:

  1. People are greedy in nature, and maximumize their benefit;
  2. People tend to compare their own situation with others (satisfied if others are worse).

Materialism

It is 1) individual value orientation and 2) personality traits.

來自於少年時期對自己的不明確，而安全感則來自於擁有別人擁有的東西。但是這種滿足並不總是管用的，所以有了物質主義的性格。

Definition & “core-extended self model”

Belief that consumption is the rout to happiness, meaning and the solution to most personal problems.

( extended self ( core value ) )
↑ Materialism is a part of extended self.

通過MT，可以讓對方馬上發現其優點而非如學識般需要發掘。

男人作為雄性動物，物質可以成為他的“羽毛”。

The Nature

  1. Material possessions as an expression of the extended self (Belk, 1988)
  2. Balance between the public and private choice

    e.g., $6000 tax refund to poor or ipad?
    Every choice you make is from your extended self.

The Motivation

Two types of reasons motivate us to materialistic:

  1. When psychological needs of individuals are not met, they tend to move towards materialism as a type of compensatory strategy to lesson the distressing effects of insecurity.

    e.g., 女友不開心刷卡的行為，來源於對自己懷疑而導致的自尊心下降，通過消費可以提高自尊。

  2. Consumers (including young people) develop materialistic value orientation from exposure to materialistic models and values.

    Ads wanna transmit a thought that “everyone got one, what about you?”
    e.g., Fur products (以其他更高層次哺乳動物的犧牲獲取）.

The High Price

A long term effect: MT has a negative correlation with happiness.
Adults who focused on money, image, and fame reported less self-actualization and vitality, and more depressed than those less concerned with these values.
reference point will change once you reach one.

Hong Kong - a materialistic society

  1. materialistic values prevail in the mass media;

    Media reports how much a wedding cost, how much the dress cost, etc.
    Media reports lifestyles of the rich and famous;

  2. The fashion business is economically significant in Hong Kong.

A Question Arises as “how come even negative consequences”

Answers from Vincent:

  1. Insecrurity as the primary concern;
  2. Misinterpretation as the social motive;
  3. In sociology domain, it is useful for compromising the society

    在固化的層級中，人們可以通過matierialism作為捷徑通往上層。

    Maslow’s Hierarchy of Needs - in a healthy society, the materialism should be in level 4.

Use Materialism in Ads

To be ethical and to be rich.

  1. 不能對消費者強調MT是core value裡面的；
  2. 打破第四面牆，說產品與MT無關，只是說產品能對自身有改善。
