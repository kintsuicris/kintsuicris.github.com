1 ) Functional unity: “Any element’s function is its contribution to the total social life”

Conclusion

  1. Functionalism is a good explanation of consequences;

  2. It provides a language to describe media and society;

  3. The paradigm emphasizes consensus rather than conflict;

  4. It is a conservative theory in favor of status quo and gradual rather than radical social change;

  5. It stresses the system’s power and capacity to adjust itself in face of change (e.g., the loop of dynasty changes);

  6. It is not a particularly strong theory in explaining dramatic social change;

  7. It is also quite weak in explaining micro-level cognition and behaviors.
