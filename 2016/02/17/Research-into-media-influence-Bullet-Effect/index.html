Two-step flow theory

Media Message

Selection (always with Criteria)

Prior belief (consistance with what believe in mind)

Opinion Leaders

Any one who received some media news or info can be OL
When start to spread ideas, become a True OL

Opinion Followers

Reinforce opinion

Critique of two-step flow theory

The charm of the theory is also its downfall: selective exposure

  1. Reinforcement is a stronger effect than conversion

    is not limited effect

    argument stopper: God

  2. Is “selective” really among true choices

    e.g., Democrat vs. Republican, life will be the same
    “force selection” everywhere

  3. The flow from OL to OF represents the relay of consistent bias which strengthens reinforcement of the message for both OL and OF

    From powerful to magical
    每一下層受到的影響更加大，因為加了上一層的個人觀點

  4. Assumes hierarchy of knowledge

    collapse after new media developed

    Broccoli price vs. National day

  5. Belies the duel between mass and interpersonal communication

Horizontal relation missing in the model

  1. In-group interaction within OL and OF

    啱嘴型
    may be even more important than relations between levels

  2. Lack of cross-level interaction

  3. OL and OF are likely to be members of different SES (socioeconomic status 社會經濟地位）, hence K-gap

    who stands in high SES, he’s opinion will be in higher level.
    this is how come the hierarchy of knowledge
